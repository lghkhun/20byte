# DOC 19 — Future Billing Architecture
File: DOC_19_BILLING_FUTURE.md

Product: 20byte

Purpose: Define monetization model for future phases.

This system will power the business model of the platform.

---

# 1. Billing Philosophy

Billing must be:

simple  
transparent  
usage-based

Users should clearly understand their costs.

---

# 2. Subscription Plans

Example plans:

Starter  
Growth  
Enterprise

Plans may include limits for:

seats  
storage  
automation features

---

# 3. Seat-Based Pricing

Organizations pay based on number of users.

Example:

Starter

3 seats

Growth

10 seats

Enterprise

unlimited seats

---

# 4. WhatsApp Usage Billing

Future system will allow users to pay WhatsApp costs through the platform wallet.

Wallet flow:

User top up  
System deducts cost  
Meta API used

---

# 5. Ads Wallet

Advertisers can fund advertising from platform balance.

Future features:

ads management  
ads now pay later

---

# 6. AI Sales Agent Pricing

AI sales agent may use:

conversation-based pricing.

Example:

AI handles conversation → cost deducted.

---

# 7. Payment Gateway Integration

Future integration with payment gateway.

Flow:

Customer pays invoice  
Funds enter platform wallet  
User withdraws or spends balance

# 8. Wallet Model

Organization wallet fields:

balance

invoice payments

ads budget
